Pizza Margherita Recipe

Summary

This Website shall explain my favorite pizza recipe.

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500 g Flour
  • 2 Packages Yeast
  • 1 Tblspn. Salt
  • 650 ml Water
  • 800 ml Canned Tomatoes
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic

Preparation:

  • Preperate tomato sauce.
  • Preperate pizza dough.
  • Put sauce on pizza dough.
  • Bake until finished.
  • Enjoy your pizza. ;)
